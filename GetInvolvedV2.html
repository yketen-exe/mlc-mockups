Why Join the MLC

Membership built for practitioners

MLC members get access to exclusive benefits designed to support your Lean journey at every stage.

  • Discounted registration for all MLC events, including the Annual Conference
  • Access to member-only resources, toolkits, and presentation archives
  • A statewide peer network of 620+ Lean practitioners across 15+ industries
  • Monthly newsletter with Lean insights, event updates, and member spotlights

Individual: $120/year · Student: $60/year · Organizational bundles from $550 to $5,000/year
